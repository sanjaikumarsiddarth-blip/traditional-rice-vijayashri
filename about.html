About Vijayashri S

Vijayashri S from Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu has been passionately working to preserve and promote traditional rice varieties (Parambariya Arisi) for more than six years.

With extensive knowledge of traditional grains and healthy food practices, she has worked with more than 60 traditional rice varieties and continues to create awareness about their nutritional and cultural value.

Her mission is to revive traditional food habits, preserve agricultural heritage, and make healthy foods accessible to modern families.
