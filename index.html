Heritage Grains & Their Benefits

Traditional Tamil Nadu rice varieties are powerhouse ingredients packed with medicinal benefits, antioxidants, and minerals.

🌾 Mappillai Samba (Bridegroom Rice)

Historically given to youth for strength and stamina. It is rich in iron, zinc, and fiber, helping boost immunity, energy, and digestive health.

🌾 Karuppu Kavuni (Black Emperor Rice)

A highly valued ancient black rice loaded with anthocyanin antioxidants. It supports healthy blood sugar control, builds immunity, and aids in body detoxification.

🌾 Thooyamalli (Pure Jasmine Rice)

A fragrant, beautiful white rice variety. It keeps the nervous system strong, is exceptionally easy to digest, and is rich in essential nutrients for growing kids.

🌾 Poongar (Women's Rice)

A traditional red rice variety that is highly beneficial for women's wellness. It contains essential minerals like iron and manganese, helping restore hormonal balance.
