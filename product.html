Traditional Rice Varieties 🌾

Explore our selection of over 80+ organic traditional rice varieties and nutritious homemade mixes.

Karuppu Kavuni

Ancient black rice, traditionally served in royal feasts. Exceptionally rich in anthocyanin antioxidants, fiber, and iron.

Mappillai Samba

A heritage red rice known for infusing high physical energy and stamina. Rich in iron, zinc, and dietary fiber.

Karunguruvai

A legendary medicinal rice variety in Siddha medicine. Known for building high immunity and improving strength.

Thooyamalli

A pure, fragrant white rice grain resembling jasmine buds. Highly digestible, nutritious, and ideal for daily meals.

Poongar

A wonderful traditional red rice variety, highly recommended for women's nutrition and overall wellness.

Kullakar

A resilient traditional red rice variety rich in iron, zinc, and minerals. Perfect for preparing nutritious idlis and dosas.

Seeraga Samba

The king of aromatic rice varieties in South India. Known for its small grains, unique fragrance, and use in biryanis.

Kichili Samba

An easy-to-digest traditional raw/boiled white rice. An excellent alternative for white rice in daily diets.

Protein Health Mix

A premium multi-grain health drink powder, packed with protein and nutrients, prepared in authentic hygienic methods.

Sathu Maavu

A time-tested, nutrient-dense traditional health mix powder containing sprouted grains, millets, and pulses.

Traditional Rice Flour

Stone-ground, highly pure flour made from heritage rice varieties. Perfect for traditional rice idiyappam, kolukattai, and dosas.

Millets

A healthy selection of native millets (Varagu, Thinai, Samai, Kuthiraivali) high in protein and gluten-free fibers.

Homemade Health Powders

A range of nutrient-rich herbal mixes and seed powders prepared with authentic ingredients for optimal health.
