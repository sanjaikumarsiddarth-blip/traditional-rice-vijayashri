About Vijayashri S

Vijayashri S, from Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, has been passionately working for more than six years to preserve and promote traditional rice varieties (Parambariya Arisi). With deep knowledge and experience in traditional agriculture and food heritage, she is dedicated to bringing back the healthy grains that were widely used by our ancestors.

She currently works with more than 80 varieties of traditional rice, each known for its unique nutritional value, medicinal benefits, and cultural significance. Through her efforts, many people are rediscovering the importance of traditional foods and healthier eating habits.

Her dedication to traditional agriculture, environmental awareness, and community welfare has earned her several recognitions and awards. She has received prestigious honors including the Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Award and the Nel Jayaraman Award in Thiruthuraipoondi. In addition, she has received multiple recognitions for her contributions to social and ecological activities such as tree planting and environmental protection, along with international appreciation for her work in preserving traditional food culture.

Apart from promoting traditional rice, Vijayashri S is also known for creating unique traditional recipes using these grains. To make healthy traditional foods accessible for modern families, she prepares a variety of nutritious homemade mixes and flours, including:

  • Protein Health Mix
  • Sathu Maavu (Multi-grain powder)
  • Traditional Rice Flours (Stone-ground Maavu)
  • Other custom-formulated nutritious traditional mixes

Each product is prepared with extreme care while strictly maintaining authentic preparation methods and maximum nutritional value. Her mission is to revive traditional rice varieties, promote natural and healthy food habits, and preserve the agricultural wisdom passed down through generations.

Our Core Mission

Guiding values that shape our preservation work and agricultural goals.

Preserve Heritage

Protect and promote traditional Tamil Nadu rice varieties from seed loss and hybridization.

Healthy Living

Encourage healthier dietary choices through high-fiber, mineral-dense heritage grains.

Food Awareness

Educate communities and school children about the health and medicinal benefits of heritage crops.

Community Impact

Support local organic farmers, plant native trees, and lead environmental conservation efforts.
